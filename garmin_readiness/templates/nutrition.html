How this plan works

The Principles

🥩
Protein First

160–180g daily protects muscle mass during calorie restriction — critical over 50. Every meal is built around a protein anchor: Greek yogurt, chicken, eggs, fish, or lean beef.

🔋
Carbs Around Rides

Carbohydrates are timed to support training. Ride days get more carbs — especially before Sunday's long ride. Rest days are lower carb naturally, without rigid restriction.

💷
Budget Tesco Picks

Breakfast and lunch are built around Tesco Everyday Value / own-brand items. Targeting £3–4/day for both meals combined. Consistent repeatable choices reduce decision fatigue.

🍳
No Cooking at Work

All breakfasts and lunches require zero cooking or heating. Overnight oats, Greek yogurt pots, pre-made wraps, deli items, and ready-to-eat protein sources only.

🥗
Gousto for Dinners

Gousto provides structure, portion control, and variety for evenings. The plan specifies recipe types and calorie bands to hit nightly targets. Specific recipes rotate each week.

☀️
Sunday = Fuel Up

Long ride Sundays need +250–300 kcal vs rest days. Bigger breakfast, on-bike fuelling, and a more substantial Gousto dinner in the evening to kick off the Monday recovery.

Week 4 — Recovery Week. Reduce calories slightly.

Week 4 is the recovery week in the training plan — lower volume, lower intensity. Calories drop slightly to match the reduced training load. Rest days drop to ~1,850 kcal. Sunday ride is shorter so Sunday drops to ~2,100 kcal. This also gives the metabolism a slight reset before the next 4-week cycle.

On-bike nutrition

Tesco On-Bike Fuel Guide

45–60 min rides (Tue/Thu)

Minimal fuelling needed

  • Before: Banana or 2 oatcakes 30–45 min prior
  • During: Water only — no food needed under 60 min
  • After: Tesco chocolate milk (300ml) within 20 min
  • ~600 kcal burned — breakfast covers it
Saturday 60–90 min rides

Light fuelling

  • Before: Full Saturday breakfast, 90 min prior
  • During: 1 banana at 45 min (~23g carbs)
  • After: Chocolate milk immediately post-ride
  • ~700–900 kcal burned total
Sunday long rides (90 min–3 hrs)

Full fuelling protocol

  • Before: Big porridge breakfast 2–2.5 hrs prior
  • During: 40g carbs every 45 min — see options below
  • After: Chocolate milk + recovery plate within 45 min
  • ~900–1,400 kcal burned on 2–3 hr rides
Tesco on-bike options (~40g carbs each)

Cheap on-bike food picks

  • Banana — 23g carbs, ~90p for a bunch
  • Jaffa Cakes ×3 — 35g carbs, easy to eat riding
  • Nature Valley bar — 38g carbs, ~60p
  • Tesco malt loaf slice — 38g carbs, very cheap
  • Mini malt loaf — individual pouches, ~30g carbs
  • Tesco dried mango strips — 35g carbs, 30g pack
