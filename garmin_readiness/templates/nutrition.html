12-Week Training Plan

Nutrition

Tesco breakfasts & lunches · Gousto dinners · 160 g+ protein daily · ~0.5 kg/wk loss

Protein first

160–180 g daily protects muscle over 50. Every meal anchored: Greek yogurt, chicken, eggs, fish, or lean beef.

Carbs around rides

Carbs are timed to training. Ride days and long rides get more; rest days are naturally lower without rigid restriction.

No cooking at work

All breakfasts and lunches need zero prep: overnight oats, Greek yogurt pots, ready wraps, deli proteins.

Gousto dinners

Gousto provides structure, portion control, and variety. Use calorie bands as a guide when choosing weekly recipes.

Budget Tesco picks

Breakfast + lunch target £3–4/day combined. Consistent repeatable choices reduce decision fatigue mid-training-block.

Long ride: fuel up

Sunday long rides need the biggest breakfast, on-bike carbs every 45 min, and a recovery plate immediately after.

On-bike nutrition

45–60 min rides

  • Before: Banana or 2 oatcakes, 30–45 min prior
  • During: Water only — no food needed
  • After: Chocolate milk 300 ml within 20 min

60–90 min rides

  • Before: Full breakfast 90 min prior
  • During: 1 banana at 45 min (~23 g carbs)
  • After: Chocolate milk immediately

90 min–3 hrs

  • Before: Big porridge 2–2.5 hrs prior
  • During: 40 g carbs every 45 min
  • After: Chocolate milk + recovery plate
