·Shared Protein Base

Every flavour below starts from this mix in a 350 ml jar (except the standalone GCN Banoffee). Berries and fruit toppings go in a separate small pot — that keeps the full whey scoop and 90 g yogurt in the jar (~42 g protein). Full Sunday batch steps live on the Cook Once page (#05).

High-Protein Overnight Oats — Base Mix
1 jar~42g P2 min
Per 350 ml jar
  • 50 g porridge oats
  • 1 scoop whey or casein (~30 g powder) — keep full scoop
  • 120 ml milk
  • 90 g Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Pinch of salt
Method
  1. Mix oats, protein powder and chia in the jar; stir to distribute the powder.
  2. Add milk, yogurt, honey and salt; stir until no dry patches remain (thick but pourable).
  3. Seal and refrigerate. Add fruit from a separate topping pot when you eat.
Post-ride: eat the full jar within 45–60 min of finishing (after chocolate milk). Weekday desk: stir topping through at your desk — not a pre-ride breakfast on long-ride mornings.

On Sunday, build 3 base jars + 3 separate topping pots (~8 min hands-on). No schedule to track on recovery rides — but for weekday variety, this rotation works well:

Standalone dessert-style · post-ride treat

GCN Banoffee Pie Overnight Oats

Emma's layered banoffee from GCN — no shared base or whey scoop. Higher carbs, lower protein (~29 g from yogurt). Best as an occasional post-ride treat, not a weekday desk default.

Banoffee Overnight Oats — GCN
1 jar~29g P5 min prepGCN recipe
Ingredients
  • 90 g quick oats (about 1 cup)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Few drops of vanilla essence
  • 30 g muscovado sugar (2 tbsp — 1 tbsp in oats, 1 tbsp in yogurt)
  • 1 espresso (~50 ml)
  • 200 ml skimmed milk
  • 1 medium ripe banana (~100 g peeled), sliced
  • 150 g natural low-fat Greek-style yogurt
  • 10 g dark chocolate, grated (1 square — optional)
Method
  1. Add oats, salt, espresso, vanilla and 1 tbsp muscovado to a jar or tall glass; stir in the milk.
  2. Layer sliced banana on top — do not stir (you want distinct banoffee layers).
  3. Mix the remaining 1 tbsp muscovado into the yogurt; pour over the banana — again, don't stir.
  4. Seal and fridge overnight. Oats soak up the liquid and go creamy; yogurt sets as a toffee topping.
  5. Grate dark chocolate on top before eating. Eat the full jar within 45–60 min of finishing (after chocolate milk).
Standalone recipe — ~760 kcal · ~132 g carbs. Pair with chocolate milk first; add a whey shake if you need to hit 30 g+ protein after a long ride.
