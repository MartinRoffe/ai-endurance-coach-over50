How This Plan Works

THE PRINCIPLES

🥩
Protein First

185–200g daily (2.0–2.2 g/kg at ~92 kg) protects muscle over 50. Every meal anchored to a protein source. Greek-yogurt portion at breakfast + lunch Mon–Thu; home-mixed whey shake Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat. Saturday: chicken lunch (not eggs) + yogurt post-ruck + evening shake. Eggs on the weekend.

🔋
Carbs Around Training

Batch-cooked rice and pasta lunches on training and ride days. Banana pre-session. Paprika batch rice + prawns Thursday fuels evening kettlebell. For the early weekend starts, the carb-rich Saturday griddle dinner — not breakfast — fuels Sunday's long ride.

💷
Bulk Staples

Batch-roasted chicken, eggs, a 1kg Greek-yogurt tub, a 1kg whey tub, 1kg oats and 1kg basmati replace single-serve pots, bottles and bars. ~£30/week vs ~£48 — same protein, ~£18 saved.

🍳
Dinners: Gousto + Griddle

Gousto on the four weekday evenings (Mon–Thu). The Blackstone griddle handles Fri/Sat/Sun through summer — griddled chicken, lean steak or salmon + veg + rice/flatbreads. Hit the same 65g P / 730 kcal the Gousto picks did.

☀️
Sunday = Max Fuel

Carb-rich Saturday griddle dinner is the real pre-ride meal. 100–150 kcal fast carbs on waking. Fuel on-bike from minute 0 at 60–90 g/hr. The big porridge breakfast becomes the post-ride recovery meal — chocolate milk first. Full protocols in the Ride Fuelling guide.

≥2 h baseline — scales up

Long-ride calories scale with duration. The ~2,700 kcal baseline assumes a ~2 h ride. Add 150–200 kcal per ride-hour beyond 2 h, taken mostly as on-bike carbs: ~2,700 at 2 h · ~3,050 at 3.5 h · ~3,400+ at 5 h. A 5 h Z2 ride burns ~2,500–3,000 kcal — fixing Sunday at 2,350 puts you 1,500+ kcal down on your biggest day and blunts the very adaptations weeks 9–11 exist to build.

Gut training: the gut is trainable, and 60–90 g/hr only works if you rehearse it. Weeks 5–8, practise 60 g/hr on every ride ≥75 min. Weeks 9–11, push to 75–90 g/hr on the long rides so the stomach is ready for Tenerife and the 190 km charity day. Use your exact race-day mix now — no new products on event day.

Recovery Week (W4): Drop targets by ~150–200 kcal. 1–2 snacks instead of 3. Lighter dinner (Gousto Mon–Thu / lighter griddle Fri). Protein holds at 185g — in a calorie deficit protein must hold or rise; take the cut from carbs and fat only.
