# Weekend Fuelling Plan — Simplified

In-ride solids are for **weekend long rides only**. Weekday 60 min rides: **banana 45 min before, nothing on the bike.**

Full methods: **Nutrition → Ride Fuelling** (`/nutrition/fuelling`), **Recipes → Overnight Oats** (`/nutrition/recipes/overnight-oats`) and **Weekend Ride Fuel** (`/nutrition/recipes/weekend-fuel`).

## How many rice cakes?

Prep **Friday evening** (rice cakes need ~24 h in the fridge). Use the planned Sunday ride length from your training plan.

| Planned ride | Rice cakes to make | Electrolyte bottles | Also pack |
|---|---|---|---|
| ≤1.5 h (recovery) | 2 | E1 | Half banana before |
| 2 h | 4 | E1 | — |
| 2.5–3 h | 6 | E1 + E2 | — |
| 3.5–4 h | 8 | E2 (+ café refill) | Flapjack piece |
| 4.5–5 h | 10 | E2 + E3 | 1 flapjack bar |
| 5.5–6 h | 12 | E3 + E4 | 1 flapjack bar |

**Rule of thumb:** 2 rice cakes per ride-hour + 1 spare (~27 g carbs each → ~55 g/hr).

**One recipe:** standard bacon & egg rice cakes (or cinnamon sweet every other weekend for variety). See `/nutrition/recipes/weekend-fuel#standard-rice-cake`.

## On the ride

1. **Before:** half banana or one rice cake square (10–15 min before rolling).
2. **On the bike:** rice cakes from minute 0; sip electrolyte bottles (no carb powder in bottles).
3. **Recovery:** chocolate milk in the car → protein overnight oats jar within 60 min (build jar the night before).

## Electrolyte bottle (per 500 ml)

- 500 ml water (or 300 ml water + 200 ml coconut water)
- ¼ tsp table salt
- Pinch lite salt
- 1 tsp honey
- Juice of ½ lemon

Label **E1**, **E2**, etc. Friday eve.

## What we dropped

- Weekend A/B rotation (which cake when, which oats flavour)
- Energy balls as standing prep (optional for 5 h+ only)
- Weekday on-bike fuel (banana pre-ride is enough for 60 min)

Pick any recovery overnight oats flavour post-ride — see `/nutrition/recipes/overnight-oats` (EF Pro, Strawberries, Classic, Quick Banoffee, GCN Banoffee, or plain berries).
